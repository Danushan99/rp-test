SRI LANKA PORTS AUTHORITY (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT
Arrangement of Sections
1.	Short title.
2.	Grant of pension to employees of Colombo Port Commission absorbed by the Ports Authority and not eligible for pension under section 17 of the Sri Lanka Ports Authority Act.
3.	Condition to be satisfied before award is made.
4.	Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency.
36 of 1990.
AN ACT
to make special provision to enable employees of the Colombo Port Commission who were absorbed by the Sri Lanka Ports Authority and who did not qualify for the grant of a pension, to be eligible for the grant of a pension; and to provide for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
