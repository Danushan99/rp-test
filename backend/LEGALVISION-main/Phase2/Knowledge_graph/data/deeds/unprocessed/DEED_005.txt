TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the said allotment of land hereby sold granted conveyed and assured or expressed or intended so to be together with all the rights and appurtenances thereunto belonging unto the VENDEE absolutely and forever.
AND the said VENDOR doth hereby covenant promise and agree with the said VENDEE, that the said VENDEE shall and may at all times hereafter peaceably and quietly possess and enjoy the said allotments of land hereby sold and conveyed and receive the rents income issues and profits thereof without any interruption or disturbance by the said VENDOR or any other person or persons whomsoever lawfully claiming any right or title thereto and that the said VENDOR has good right and full power and lawful and absolute authority to transfer and convey the said allotment of land morefully described in the Schedule hereto in the manner aforesaid and that the same are free from all encumbrances whatsoever and that the said VENDOR shall and will at all times hereafter warrant and defend the title to the said allotment of land morefully described in the Schedule hereto unto the said VENDEE against any and every person or persons whomsoever.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF the said VENDOR doth hereunto and to two others from the same tenor and date as these presents set his hand at Negombo on this Twentieth (20th) day of January Two Thousand and Twenty One (2021).
