01. The VENDOR shall sell and the PURCHASER shall purchase in his name or in the name of his nominee all that land and premises depicted as Lot 3 of the said Plan No.8547 fully described in the Schedule hereto (sometimes hereinafter called and referred to as the said premises) free of encumbrances whatsoever for the price or sum of RUPEES TWO MILLION THREE HUNDRED THOUSAND (Rs.2,300,000/-) within the time frame and in the manner and mode set out hereinafter.
The PURCHASER shall pay to the VENDOR a sum of RUPEES SEVEN HUNDRED AND FIFTY THOUSAND (Rs.750,000/-)as an advance prior to or at the execution of this Agreement to be paid at the time of the execution of this agreement

On the payment of the balance purchase price of RUPEES ONE MILLION FIVE HUNDRED AND FIFTY THOUSAND (Rs.1,550,000/-)to the VENDOR by the PURCHASER within six months from the date hereof the VENDOR shall execute a Deed of Transfer conveying the said premises to the PURCHASER or his nominee as the case may be.

On the execution of this Agreement and receipt of SEVEN HUNDRED AND FIFTY THOUSAND (Rs.750,000/=) the VENDOR shall not encumber the said premises or any part thereof by pledging, mortgaging or hypothecating or otherwise. 

05. The VENDOR doth hereby declare with and assure unto the PURCHASER that:-
5.1 The VENDOR hath good and valid title to convey the said premises as described in the Schedule hereto or as may be described in accordance with the Plan.
5.2 There are no pledges or mortgages hypothecation or liens affecting the said premises or any part thereof. 
5.3 There are no cases or proceedings affecting the said premises pending before any court or Tribunal or Authority. 
06. The VENDOR once advance payment is received from the PURCHASER shall:
6.1 Shall hand over peaceful and vacant possession of the premises to the PURCHASER.
6.2 Shall pay all rates, taxes, and electricity and water bills up to the date of the execution of this deed in relation to the said premises.
07. The PURCHASER shall complete the purchase of the said premises on or before the Tenth(10th) day of September Two Thousand and Seventeen (2017).
08. It is mutually further agreed between the VENDOR and PURCHASER:
8.1 On the PURCHASER observing and performing the several terms and conditions herein contained and the VENDOR neglecting and failing to execute the deed of transfer the PURCHASER at his option shall be entitled to:- 
8.1(i) Enforce specific performance of the VENDOR'S covenant to sell and convey the said premises to the PURCHASER or his nominee(s).
 Or
8.1(ii) Recover the moneys paid to the VENDOR under this Agreement together with interest thereon at the rate of Twenty two Percentum (22%) per mensum till the date of payment of such moneys.
On the VENDOR observing and performing the several conditions and stipulations herein contained and the PURCHASER neglecting and failing to complete the purchase on or before Tenth(10TH) day of September Two Thousand and Seventeen (2017) the VENDOR shall dorfeit RUPEES SEVEN HUNDRED AND FIFTY THOUSAND (Rs.750,000/-)of the advance payment made hereunder
8.3 (i) Subject to above the PURCHASER shall take all steps to pay the balance amount of RUPEES SEVEN HUNDRED AND FIFTY THOUSAND (Rs.750,000/-)within three (3) months but non payment within Six (6) months shall make the Purchaser liable for damages and interest as stated in column 8.2 hereof. 
09. All communications notices if any given hereunder shall be deemed sufficiently served if made in writing and sent under registered cover post to the respective addresses herein at the beginning given

IN WITNESS WHEREOF the VENDOR and the PURCHASER have hereunto and to two others of the same tenor and date as these presents set their respective hands at the place on the date month and year at the beginning hereof written.
