No. 100, Negombo Road, Ja-Ela.
Prior Registration – L.R.O. Gampaha Folio S/45/120

MORTGAGE BOND
No. 1210
TO ALL TO WHOM THESE PRESENTS shall come LANKA APPARELS (PRIVATE) LIMITED, a Company duly registered under the provisions of the Companies Act No. 7 of 2007 bearing registration No. PV 98765, having its registered office at EPZ, Katunayake (hereinafter called and referred to as “the Mortgagor” which term or expression shall where the context so requires or admits mean and include the said LANKA APPARELS (PRIVATE) LIMITED its successors in office and assigns).
S E N D G R E E T I N G S
WHEREAS:
a) The Mortgagor is seized and possessed of the land and premises described in the Schedule hereto, which houses its factory and warehouse complex.
b) The Mortgagor, by a resolution of its Board of Directors dated 20/10/2025, resolved to obtain a business expansion facility from LANKA ORIX LEASING COMPANY PLC (LOLC), a public limited company having its registered office at No. 100/1, Sri Jayawardenepura Mawatha, Rajagiriya (hereinafter called and referred to as “the Mortgagee”) in a sum of Rupees Fifty Million (Rs. 50,000,000/-) and to mortgage the property in the Schedule hereto as security therefor.
NOW KNOW YE AND THESE PRESENTS WITNESSETH that the Mortgagor does hereby covenant with the Mortgagee to pay all sums due under the said facility.
AND as security for such payment, the Mortgagor does hereby specially mortgage and hypothecate to and with the said Mortgagee as a primary mortgage the property described in the Schedule hereto (hereinafter referred to as “the mortgaged property”).
AND the Mortgagor does hereby covenant with the Mortgagee as follows:
That the Mortgagor has full power and authority under its Articles of Association and the aforesaid resolution to mortgage the said property in the manner aforesaid.
That the mortgaged property is free from all encumbrances, seizures, and claims whatsoever.
That the Mortgagor shall not, without the prior written consent of the Mortgagee, sell, lease, or create any further charge over the mortgaged property.
That in the event of default in payment or breach of any covenant herein, or if proceedings are taken for the winding up of the Mortgagor, all monies secured hereunder shall become immediately due and payable, and the Mortgagee shall be entitled to enforce this security.
That the total sum recoverable under this bond is limited to the principal of Rupees Fifty Million (Rs. 50,000,000/-) and any interest, costs, and charges accrued thereon.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF the Common Seal of the said LANKA APPARELS (PRIVATE) LIMITED is hereunto affixed at Katunayake on this Fifth (5th) day of November Two Thousand and Twenty-Five (2025).
