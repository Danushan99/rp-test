I, GEORGE ALMEIDA (holder of National Identity Card No. 390011223V) of No. 35, Lighthouse Street, Galle Fort, Galle in the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka being of sound mind memory and understanding declare my intention to make and execute my Last Will and Testament.
I DO HEREBY REVOKE CANCEL AND ANNUL all former Wills Codicils and other writings or dispositions of a Testamentary nature heretofore made and done executed by me and declare this to be my LAST WILL and TESTAMENT.
I DO HEREBY NOMINATE CONSTITUTE AND APPOINT my son PETER ALMEIDA (holder of National Identity Card No. 652233445V) of the same address to be the sole Executor of this my LAST WILL and TESTAMENT hereby giving and granting him all such power and authority as are required or allowed in Law.
I the said GEORGE ALMEIDA DO HEREBY GIVE DEVISE and BEQUEATH to my beloved wife MARY ALMEIDA a life interest in my house and property bearing assessment No. 35, Lighthouse Street, Galle Fort, Galle, to enjoy the rents and profits thereof during her lifetime without the power of sale or mortgage, and upon her demise, I give devise and bequeath the same property to my two children PETER ALMEIDA and ANNE DIAS in equal shares.
I the said GEORGE ALMEIDA DO HEREBY GIVE DEVISE and BEQUEATH to my son PETER ALMEIDA my collection of antique furniture and my library of books.
I the said GEORGE ALMEIDA DO HEREBY GIVE DEVISE and BEQUEATH to my daughter ANNE DIAS all my shares held in John Keells Holdings PLC.
I the said GEORGE ALMEIDA DO HEREBY GIVE DEVISE and BEQUEATH the rest residue and remainder of all my properties estate and effects whatsoever and wheresoever situate including all monies in banks to my said children PETER ALMEIDA and ANNE DIAS in EQUAL SHARES.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF I the said GEORGE ALMEIDA do hereby set my hand to this my Last Will and another of same tenor and date as these presents at Galle aforesaid on this 30th day of July Two Thousand and Twenty One.
SIGNED AND DECLARED by the above named Testator GEORGE ALMEIDA as and for his LAST WILL And TESTAMENT in the presence of us the Undersigned Witnesses who in his presence and at his request and in the presence of one another all being present together at the same time have subscribed our names hereto as attesting witnesses.
