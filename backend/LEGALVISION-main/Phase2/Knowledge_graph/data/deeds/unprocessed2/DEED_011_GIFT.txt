 KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS THAT (1) ANUSHA DHARMAWARDENA and (2) ANIKA DHARMAWARDENA both of No. 14, Kawdana Road, Dehiwala (hereinafter called and referred to as the Donors) in consideration of the natural love and affection which the said Donors have and bear unto (1) YUNEKA DHARMAWARDENA and (2) KUSUMI DHARMAWARDENA (the father and the mother respectively of the said Donors) also both of No. 14, Kawdana Road, Dehiwala (hereinafter called and referred to as the Donees) and for diverse other good causes and considerations hereunto specially moving do hereby give grant convey assign transfer set over and assure unto the said Donees during their respective natural lives as a gift inter vivos absolute and irrevocable, subject to the life interest of the Donors, the full and free right to possess take receive and enjoy the rent income and profits of the properties in the Schedule hereto fully described and together with all rights privileges easements servitudes and appurtenances whatsoever to the said premises belonging or used or enjoyed therewith or reputed or known as part and parcel thereof and all the estate right title interest property claim and demand whatsoever of the said Donors of in to upon or out of the same during the respective natural lives of the said Donees.

 TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the said full and free right of the said Donors to possess take receive and enjoy the rent income and profits of the said premises in the Schedule hereto fully described which are of the value of RUPEES TWENTY FIVE THOUSAND (Rs. 25 000/-) unto the said Donees during their respective natural lives.

AND the said Donors do hereby for themselves their and each of their respective heirs executors and administrators covenant and declare to and with the said Donees that the said Donors now have good right to convey the said full and free right to possess take receive and enjoy the rent income and profits of the premises in the Schedule hereto fully described in the manner aforesaid and that the same are free from encumbrances whatsoever and that the said Donors and their aforewritten have not made done or committed or been party or privy to any act deed matter or thing whereby or by reason whereof the said premises or any part or portion thereof are is can shall or may be impeached or encumbered in title charge estate or otherwise howsoever and that the said Donors and their aforewritten shall and will at all times hereafter warrant and defend the title to the said premises in the Schedule hereto fully described and every part or portion thereof unto the said Donees during their respective natural lives against any person or persons whomsoever and that the said Donors and their aforewritten shall at the request cost and expense of the said Donees during their respective natural lives do and execute or cause to be done and executed all such further and other acts deeds assurances matters and other things whatsoever which may be necessary or expedient for the better and more perfectly and effectually conveying and assuring the said rights or any part or portion thereof unto the said Donees as may be reasonably required.

 AND the said Donees (1) YUNEKA DHARMAWARDENA and (2) KUSUMI DHARMAWARDENA do hereby thankfully accept the gift hereby made. 

 IN WITNESS WHEREOF the said Donors and the said Donees do hereunto and to two others of the same tenor and date as these presents set their respective hands at Nugegoda in Colombo on this Twenty-Eighth (28th ) day of August Two Thousand and Seventeen (2017).
