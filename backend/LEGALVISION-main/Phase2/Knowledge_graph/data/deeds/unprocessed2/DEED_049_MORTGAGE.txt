MORTGAGE BOND
No. 155
TO ALL TO WHOM THESE PRESENTS shall come (1) ANURA BANDARA (holder of NIC No. 781231234V) and (2) NIMALEE BANDARA (holder of NIC No. 805675678V), husband and wife, both of No. 15, Asgiriya, Kandy (hereinafter jointly and severally called and referred to as “the Mortgagors” which term or expression shall mean and include the said ANURA BANDARA and NIMALEE BANDARA and their respective heirs, executors and administrators).
S E N D G R E E T I N G S
WHEREAS:
a) The Mortgagors are jointly seized and possessed of the property described in the Schedule hereto.
b) The Mortgagors have requested HATTON NATIONAL BANK PLC, a public limited company having its registered office at HNB Towers, No. 479, T. B. Jayah Mawatha, Colombo 10 (hereinafter called and referred to as “the Bank”) for a housing loan facility of Rupees Fifteen Million (Rs. 15,000,000/-), which the Bank has agreed to grant upon the security of a primary mortgage over the said property.
NOW KNOW YE AND THESE PRESENTS WITNESSETH that the Mortgagors do hereby jointly and severally covenant with the Bank to repay the said facility with interest as per the terms agreed upon.
AND as security for the due repayment of the said monies, the Mortgagors do hereby specially mortgage and hypothecate to and with the Bank, as a primary mortgage free from all encumbrances, the property described in the Schedule hereto (hereinafter referred to as “the mortgaged property”).
AND the Mortgagors do hereby covenant with the Bank as follows:
That they have good and legal right to mortgage the said property.
That they shall not sell, gift, lease or otherwise encumber the mortgaged property without the prior written consent of the Bank.
That they shall insure the property against fire and other risks and punctually pay all taxes and outgoings.
That in the event of default, the Bank shall be entitled to pursue all legal remedies available against the Mortgagors jointly and severally for the recovery of all sums due.
This Bond shall secure a maximum principal sum of Rupees Fifteen Million (Rs. 15,000,000/-) together with accrued interest and costs.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF the said Mortgagors have set their respective hands hereunto and to two others of the same tenor as these Presents in Kandy on this Twentieth (20th) day of January Two Thousand and Twenty-Six (2026).
