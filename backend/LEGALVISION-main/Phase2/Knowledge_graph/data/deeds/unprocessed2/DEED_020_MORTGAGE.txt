MORTGAGE BOND

TO ALL TO WHOM THESE PRESENTS shall come PIYAL GAMAGE (holder of National Identity Card No. 751112223V) of Yaddehimulla, Unawatuna, Galle (hereinafter called and referred to as “the Mortgagor” which term shall include his heirs executors and administrators).
S E N D G R E E T I N G S
WHEREAS:
a) The Mortgagor is seized and possessed of and otherwise well and sufficiently entitled to the bare land described in the Schedule hereto.
b) The Mortgagor has requested DAVID PERERA (holder of NIC No. 602223334V) of No. 5, Lighthouse Street, Galle Fort (hereinafter called and referred to as “the Mortgagee” which term shall include his heirs executors administrators and assigns) for a personal loan of Rupees Five Million (Rs. 5,000,000/-) which the Mortgagee has agreed to lend and advance upon the Mortgagor executing these presents as security for the due repayment of the said sum with interest.
NOW KNOW YE AND THESE PRESENTS WITNESSETH that the Mortgagor acknowledges receipt of the said sum of Rupees Five Million (Rs. 5,000,000/-) and covenants with the Mortgagee to repay the same.
AND as security for such repayment, the Mortgagor does hereby specially mortgage and hypothecate to and with the said Mortgagee as a primary mortgage free from all encumbrances the property described in the Schedule hereto (hereinafter referred to as “the mortgaged property”).
AND the Mortgagor does hereby covenant with the Mortgagee as follows:
That the Mortgagor has good right to mortgage the said property.
That the Mortgagor shall repay the said principal sum of Rs. 5,000,000/- in full to the Mortgagee on or before the 10th day of February 2028.
That the Mortgagor shall pay interest on the principal sum at the rate of ten percent (10%) per annum, payable annually, until the principal sum is fully settled.
That should the Mortgagor fail to repay the principal sum and any accrued interest by the due date, the Mortgagee shall be entitled to institute legal proceedings to obtain a hypothecary decree for the sale of the mortgaged property to recover all outstanding monies.
That the Mortgagor shall not sell, transfer, or encumber the mortgaged property until this mortgage is fully discharged and cancelled.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF the said Mortgagor has set his hand hereunto and to two others of the same tenor as these Presents in Galle on this Tenth (10th) day of February Two Thousand and Twenty-Six (2026).

Liyanage, SOUTH by Access Path, and WEST by Lot 2 in the same plan, containing in extent TWENTY PERCHES (A0.R0.P20.00).
