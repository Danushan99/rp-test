I, SIVAGAMI ARUMUGAM (holder of National Identity Card No. 428876543V) of No. 5, Navalar Road, Nallur, Jaffna in the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka being of sound mind memory and understanding declare my intention to make and execute my Last Will and Testament.
I DO HEREBY REVOKE CANCEL AND ANNUL all former Wills Codicils and other writings or dispositions of a Testamentary nature heretofore made and done executed by me and declare this to be my LAST WILL and TESTAMENT.
I DO HEREBY NOMINATE CONSTITUTE AND APPOINT my son ARUN ARUMUGAM (holder of National Identity Card No. 701122334V) of the same address and my daughter LAKSHMI KANAGASABAI (holder of National Identity Card No. 726655443V) of No. 15, Point Pedro Road, Jaffna to be the Joint Executors of this my LAST WILL and TESTAMENT hereby giving and granting them all such power and authority as are required or allowed in Law.
I the said SIVAGAMI ARUMUGAM DO HEREBY GIVE DEVISE and BEQUEATH to my said son ARUN ARUMUGAM and my said daughter LAKSHMI KANAGASABAI my undivided half share of the land called “Vayal” situated at Nallur in the District of Jaffna, Northern Province together with the house standing thereon in EQUAL SHARES.
I the said SIVAGAMI ARUMUGAM DO HEREBY GIVE DEVISE and BEQUEATH to my granddaughter MEENA KANAGASABAI all my gold jewellery including my “Thali Kodi”.
I the said SIVAGAMI ARUMUGAM DO HEREBY GIVE DEVISE and BEQUEATH to my grandson RAVI ARUMUGAM all my monies lying to my credit in Account No. 8090012345 of Commercial Bank, Jaffna Branch.
I the said SIVAGAMI ARUMUGAM DO HEREBY GIVE DEVISE and BEQUEATH the rest residue and remainder of all my properties estate and effects whatsoever and wheresoever situate to my said son ARUN ARUMUGAM and my said daughter LAKSHMI KANAGASABAI in EQUAL SHARES.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF I the said SIVAGAMI ARUMUGAM do hereby set my hand to this my Last Will and another of same tenor and date as these presents at Jaffna aforesaid on this 5th day of September Two Thousand and Nineteen.
SIGNED AND DECLARED by the above named Testatrix SIVAGAMI ARUMUGAM as and for her LAST WILL And TESTAMENT in the presence of us the Undersigned Witnesses who in her presence and at her request and in the presence of one another all being present together at the same time have subscribed our names hereto as attesting witnesses.
