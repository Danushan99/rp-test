THIS INDENTURE is made and entered into at Dehiwala on this Fifteenth day of May Two Thousand and Twenty-Four BETWEEN MR. ANURA BANDARA EDIRISINGHE (holder of N.I.C No. 751122345V) of No. 34, Hill Street, Dehiwala (hereinafter called and referred to as “the Lessor” which term shall where the context so requires or admits mean and include the said ANURA BANDARA EDIRISINGHE his heirs, executors, administrators and assigns) of the One Part
AND MR. KAVINDA PRASAD PERERA (holder of N.I.C. No. 880211456V) and MRS. ISHARA DILRUKSHI PERERA (holder of N.I.C No. 895432789V) both of No. 78, W.A. Silva Mawatha, Colombo 06 (hereinafter called and referred to as “the Lessees” which term shall where the context so requires or admits mean and include the said KAVINDA PRASAD PERERA and ISHARA DILRUKSHI PERERA their respective heirs, executors, administrators and assigns) of the Other Part.
WITNESSETH
WHEREAS under and by virtue of Deed of Transfer bearing No. 921 dated 10th January 2011 attested by K.L. Fernando, Notary Public of Colombo the said Lessor became the owner and seized and possessed of or otherwise well and sufficiently entitled to the premises bearing assessment No. 34/A, Hill Street, Dehiwala more fully described in the "SCHEDULE" hereto.
AND WHEREAS the said Lessor has agreed to lease and the Lessees have agreed to take on lease the premises bearing assessment No: 34/A, Hill Street, Dehiwala more fully described in the "SCHEDULE" hereto for and during the term or period of Two (02) years commencing from the 1st day of June Two thousand and Twenty-Four and ending on the 31st day of May Two Thousand and Twenty-Six subject to the terms and conditions hereinafter set forth and contained.
NOW THIS INDENTURE WITNESSES that in pursuance of the said agreement and in consideration of the rent hereinafter reserved and of the covenants on the part of the Lessees to be performed and observed the Lessor doth hereby demise and let unto the Lessees the premises bearing assessment No: 34/A, Hill Street, Dehiwala.
ON THIS INDENTURE FURTHER WITNESSETH and it is hereby mutually agreed by and between the Lessor and Lessees as follows:-
1. TO HOLD the demised premises with all rights and appurtenances thereto unto the Lessees for the full term or period of Two (02) Years commencing from 1st day of June Two thousand and Twenty-Four and ending on 31st day of May Two Thousand and Twenty-Six.
2. YIELDING AND PAYING THEREFOR by the Lessees unto the Lessor during the said term the monthly rental of Rupees One Hundred and Fifty Thousand (Rs 150,000/-) of lawful money of Sri Lanka, payable on or before the fifth (5th) day of each and every month.
3. REFUNDABLE SECURITY DEPOSIT: On the signing of this agreement, the Lessees shall make a Refundable Security Deposit amounting to Rupees Nine Hundred Thousand (Rs 900,000/-), equivalent to six months' rent, which sum shall be returned to the Lessees upon the termination of this Lease free of interest and less any arrears of rent, utility bills, or costs for damages beyond fair wear and tear.
4. THE LESSEES HEREBY COVENANT WITH THE LESSOR AS FOLLOWS :-
(a) To pay the reserved rent on the days and in the manner aforesaid.
(b) To use the demised premises for residential purposes for the Lessees and their immediate family only.
(c) To keep the interior of the demised premises in good and tenantable repair and condition.
(d) To pay all charges for electricity, water, telephone, internet, and any other utilities consumed at the premises.
(e) Not to sub-let or part with the possession of the premises or any part thereof.
(f) To permit the Lessor or his agents with prior notice to enter and inspect the premises at reasonable times.
(g) Not to make any structural alterations to the premises without the prior written consent of the Lessor.
(h) At the expiration or sooner determination of the lease, to hand over vacant possession of the premises in the same good order and condition as it was received, fair wear and tear excepted.
5. THE LESSOR DOTH HEREBY COVENANT WITH THE LESSEES AS FOLLOWS :-
(a) To pay all rates, taxes, and assessments payable in respect of the demised premises.
(b) To carry out all major structural repairs to the demised premises when necessary.
(c) That the Lessees, paying the rent and observing the covenants herein, shall peaceably and quietly hold and enjoy the demised premises during the said term without any interruption by the Lessor.
6. PROVIDED ALWAYS AND IT IS HEREBY MUTUALLY AGREED AS FOLLOWS :-
(a) If the rent shall be in arrears for thirty (30) days, or if the Lessees shall breach any covenant herein, the Lessor may re-enter the premises and thereupon this lease shall determine.
(b) The provisions of the Rent Act No. 7 of 1972 and its subsequent amendments shall not apply to the demised premises.
(c) Either party may terminate this agreement after the first six (06) months by giving three (03) months' prior written notice to the other party.
(d) Stamp duty and all other costs related to the execution of this indenture shall be borne by the Lessees.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF the parties have set their hands hereunto at Dehiwala on the date first above written.
