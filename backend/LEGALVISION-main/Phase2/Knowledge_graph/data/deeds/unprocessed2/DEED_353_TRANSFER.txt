(hereinafter referred to as the VENDOR) do hereby sell, grant, convey, assign, transfer and set over unto the VENDEE
the land described below.

WHEREAS the said Vendor is seized and possessed of the said land under and by virtue of Deed No. 221
registered at the Land Registry of Delkanda – Nugegoda.

NOW KNOW YE that the said land is bounded on the:

NORTH: by land claimed by owner under Deed No. 220
EAST: by land transferred under Deed No. 223
SOUTH: by Wijerama Mawatha
WEST: by land transferred under Deed No. 221

EXTENT: TEN PERCHES (A:0 R:0 P:10.00)

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the said allotment of land together with all rights, ways, easements, privileges and appurtenances
thereunto belonging unto the said VENDEE absolutely and forever.
