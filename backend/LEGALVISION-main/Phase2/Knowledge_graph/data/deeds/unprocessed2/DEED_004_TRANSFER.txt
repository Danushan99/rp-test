De Silva, Licensed Surveyor under and by virtue of Deed of Gift No. 500 dated 18.07.2016 attested by K. Liyanage, Notary Public which is morefully described in the schedule hereto.

NOW KNOW YE AND THESE PRESENTS WITNESS that the said VENDOR in pursuance of the said Agreement and in consideration of the said sum of RUPEES FORTY FIVE MILLION SEVEN HUNDRED AND FIFTY THOUSAND (Rs. 45,750,000/=) of lawful money of the said Republic of Sri Lanka well and truly paid to the VENDOR by the said Vendee (the receipt whereof the VENDOR do hereby expressly admit and acknowledge) doth hereby sell, grant, convey, assign, transfer, set over and assure unto the said VENDEE the said allotment of land morefully described in the Schedule hereto together with all and singular the rights, ways, privileges, easements advantages, servitudes and appurtenances whatsoever thereunto belonging or appertaining or usually held used occupied enjoyed therewith or reputed or known as part and parcel thereof.
TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the said allotment of land hereby sold granted conveyed and assured or expressed or intended so to be together with all the rights and appurtenances thereunto belonging unto the VENDEE absolutely and forever.
AND the said VENDOR doth hereby covenant promise and agree with the said VENDEE, that the said VENDEE shall and may at all times hereafter peaceably and quietly possess and enjoy the said allotments of land hereby sold and conveyed and receive the rents income issues and profits thereof without any interruption or disturbance by the said VENDOR or any other person or persons whomsoever lawfully claiming any right or title thereto and that the said VENDOR has good right and full power and lawful and absolute authority to transfer and convey the said allotment of land morefully described in the Schedule hereto in the manner aforesaid and that the same are free from all encumbrances whatsoever.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF the said VENDOR doth hereunto and to two others from the same tenor and date as these presents set her hand at Galle on this Second (2nd) day of June Two Thousand and Twenty (2020).
