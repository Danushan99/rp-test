THIS INDENTURE is made and entered into at Colombo on this Twenty-Second day of July Two Thousand and Twenty-Three BETWEEN CREST PROPERTIES (PVT) LTD, a company duly incorporated in Sri Lanka bearing registration No. PV 12345, having its registered office at No. 50, Jawatte Road, Colombo 05 (hereinafter called and referred to as “the Lessor” which term shall where the context so requires or admits mean and include the said CREST PROPERTIES (PVT) LTD its successors and assigns) of the One Part
AND TECHNOVA SOLUTIONS (PVT) LTD, a company duly incorporated in Sri Lanka bearing registration No. PV 67890, having its registered office at No. 200, Nawala Road, Rajagiriya (hereinafter called and referred to as “the Lessee” which term shall where the context so requires or admits mean and include the said TECHNOVA SOLUTIONS (PVT) LTD its successors and assigns) of the Other Part.
WITNESSETH
WHEREAS the Lessor is the absolute owner and possessed of the office premises described in the Schedule hereto, being the entire third (3rd) floor of the building bearing assessment No. 75, Galle Face Court 2, Colombo 03.
AND WHEREAS the Lessor has agreed to lease and the Lessee has agreed to take on lease the said office premises more fully described in the "SCHEDULE" hereto for a period of Three (03) years commencing from the 1st day of August Two Thousand and Twenty-Three and ending on the 31st day of July Two Thousand and Twenty-Six subject to the terms and conditions hereinafter contained.
NOW THIS INDENTURE WITNESSES that in pursuance of the said agreement and in consideration of a total sum of Rupees Eighteen Million (Rs 18,000,000/-) being the lease rental for the full period of Three (03) years payable as provided hereinafter, the Lessor doth hereby let and demise unto the Lessee the premises described in the Schedule hereto.
1. TO HOLD the demised premises unto the Lessee for the full term of Three (03) Years commencing from 1st day of August Two Thousand and Twenty-Three and ending on 31st day of July Two Thousand and Twenty-Six.
2. YIELDING AND PAYING THEREFOR the total lease rental of Rupees Eighteen Million (Rs 18,000,000/-) payable as follows:
(a) For the period 1st August 2023 to 31st July 2024 a sum of Rupees Five Million Four Hundred Thousand (Rs 5,400,000/-) to be paid at the execution of these presents.
(b) For the period 1st August 2024 to 31st July 2025 a sum of Rupees Six Million (Rs 6,000,000/-) to be paid on or before the 1st day of July 2024.
(c) For the period 1st August 2025 to 31st July 2026 a sum of Rupees Six Million Six Hundred Thousand (Rs 6,600,000/-) to be paid on or before the 1st day of July 2025.
3. REFUNDABLE SECURITY DEPOSIT: The Lessee shall pay a Refundable Security Deposit of Rupees One Million Five Hundred Thousand (Rs 1,500,000/-) upon signing this agreement, which sum shall be refunded without interest at the termination of the lease, subject to deductions for any breaches of covenants by the Lessee.
4. THE LESSEE COVENANTS WITH THE LESSOR AS FOLLOWS :-
(a) To pay the reserved rent in the manner aforesaid.
(b) To use the demised premises solely for the purpose of carrying on its business as an information technology firm.
(c) To pay all utility charges including electricity, water, and any service or maintenance fees applicable to the said premises.
(d) To keep the interior of the premises, including all fixtures and fittings, in good repair and condition.
(e) Not to sublet, assign, or part with possession of the premises without the prior written consent of the Lessor.
(f) Not to make any structural alterations or additions to the premises. Any non-structural alterations require the Lessor's prior approval.
(g) To permit the Lessor and its agents to enter the premises for inspection or repairs upon giving reasonable notice.
(h) To peacefully yield up the premises with all fixtures and fittings belonging to the Lessor in good condition upon the expiration of the term.
5. THE LESSOR COVENANTS WITH THE LESSEE AS FOLLOWS :-
(a) To pay all present and future rates, taxes, and assessments charged upon the demised premises.
(b) To maintain the exterior, structure, roof, and common areas of the building in good and substantial repair.
(c) That the Lessee performing its covenants shall peaceably enjoy the demised premises without any interruption from the Lessor.
6. PROVIDED ALWAYS AND IT IS HEREBY MUTUALLY AGREED AS FOLLOWS :-
(a) The Lessee may, on a written request made three (03) months before the expiration of the term, be granted a renewal of the lease for a further period on terms to be mutually agreed upon.
(b) If the rent is in arrears for thirty (30) days or in case of a breach of any covenant by the Lessee, the Lessor shall be entitled to re-enter the premises and terminate this lease.
(c) Stamp duty and attestation fees for this indenture shall be borne equally by the Lessor and the Lessee.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF the Common Seals of the Lessor and the Lessee have been hereunto affixed on the date first above written.
THE SCHEDULE ABOVE REFERRED TO :-
The office premises comprising the entire Third (3rd) Floor of the building bearing Assessment No. 75, Galle Face Court 2, situated at Kollupitiya, Colombo 03, within the Colombo Municipal Council, containing a floor area of Two Thousand Five Hundred (2,500) square feet, together with the right to use common areas and two (02) designated parking spaces.
