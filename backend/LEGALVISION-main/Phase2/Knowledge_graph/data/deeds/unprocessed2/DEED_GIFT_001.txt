RESERVATION OF LIFE INTEREST

1. RIGHT TO RESIDE
The Donor hereby expressly reserves unto herself for and during the term of her natural life:
The full and exclusive right to reside in and occupy the said house.
The right to peaceful and uninterrupted enjoyment of the premises.
The right to prevent eviction or interference by the Donee.

2. RIGHT TO COLLECT RENT
If the property or any part thereof is rented:
The Donor shall have full right to collect and retain all rental income during her lifetime.
The Donee shall not claim any rental income during the Donor’s lifetime.

3. MAINTENANCE DURING LIFE INTEREST
The Donor shall:
Maintain the property in reasonable repair.
Pay municipal rates and utility charges during her lifetime.

4. LIMITATION ON DONEE
During the lifetime of the Donor, the Donee shall not:
Sell, mortgage or encumber the property.
Interfere with the Donor’s life interest.
Demand possession of the property.

Upon the death of the Donor:
The life interest shall cease and determine.
The Donee shall become absolutely entitled to the said property free from all encumbrances.
Full ownership and possession shall vest in the Donee without further act or deed.

5. IRREVOCABILITY
This Gift is made voluntarily and without coercion and shall be irrevocable.

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD
The said property unto the Donee subject to the life interest reserved unto the Donor as aforesaid.
