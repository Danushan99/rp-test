WHEREAS the VENDOR is seised and possessed of the bare land more fully described in the Schedule hereto under and by virtue of Deed of Transfer No. 556 dated 15.09.2005 attested by D. L. Samson, Notary Public of Gampaha.
AND WHEREAS the VENDOR has agreed to sell the said land to the PURCHASER, which has agreed to purchase the same.
- : W I T N E S S E T H:-
The VENDOR agrees to sell and the PURCHASER agrees to purchase the land described in the Schedule hereto free of all encumbrances for the total consideration of RUPEES FIFTEEN MILLION (Rs.15,000,000/-).
The PURCHASER shall pay a sum of RUPEES ONE MILLION FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND (Rs.1,500,000/-) as an advance payment at the execution of these presents.
The balance sum of RUPEES THIRTEEN MILLION FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND (Rs.13,500,000/-) shall be paid and the purchase completed within six (06) months from the date hereof.
Upon payment of the said balance sum, the VENDOR shall execute a Deed of Transfer conveying clear and marketable title of the said premises to the PURCHASER.
The VENDOR declares that she has good title to the land, that the property is free from all encumbrances and litigation, and that vacant possession will be handed over on the date of the final transfer.
Should the VENDOR fail to complete the sale, she shall be liable to refund the advance and pay an equal sum of RUPEES ONE MILLION FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND (Rs.1,500,000/-) as liquidated damages to the PURCHASER.
Should the PURCHASER fail to complete the purchase having been offered a valid title by the VENDOR, the VENDOR shall be entitled to forfeit the advance of RUPEES ONE MILLION FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND (Rs.1,500,000/-) paid by the PURCHASER.
All costs related to the stamp duty and attestation of the final Deed of Transfer shall be borne by the PURCHASER.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF the parties have hereunto set their respective hands on the date and at the place first above mentioned.
