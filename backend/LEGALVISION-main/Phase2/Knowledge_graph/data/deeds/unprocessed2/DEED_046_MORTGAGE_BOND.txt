 S E N D G R E E T I N G S
WHEREAS:
the First and Second named Mortgagors (hereinafter jointly and severally called and referred to as the said “Mortgagors” ) are seized and possessed of and or otherwise well and sufficiently entitled to the Apartment Unit No 1C/F9/P3 fully described in the Schedule hereto (hereinafter referred to as the “Building Unit”) together with title to a proportionate undivided share in the Common Elements appurtenant to the said Building Unit as provided in the Semi Condominium Plan No.5741 dated 4th October 2008 made by M.A. Kalinga Licensed Surveyor;

the Mortgagors have requested SHANAKA WEERASNGHE (holder of Sri Lankan Passport No N0745749) of No. 23/1 Old Road, Wellawatta in the said Republic (hereinafter called and referred to as the “Mortgagee” which term or expression shall mean and include the said SHANAKA WEERASNGHE his heirs executors administrators and assigns) to purchase the Building Unit from the Mortgagors for an aggregate amount of Rupees Ten Million (Rs. 10,000,000/-) (hereinafter referred to as “the Facility”); 

the Mortgagors have agreed to complete the construction of the said Building Unit and hand over vacant possession of the said Building Unit to the Mortgagee on or before April 2015 and has agreed to enter into and execute these Presents to give and grant the security hereinafter mentioned for the purpose of securing the due completion of the said Building Unit and/or the repayment of the said Facility together with interest, costs and other charges in the event of a default, subject to the terms and conditions contained in these Presents.

NOW KNOW YE AND THESE PRESENTS WITNESSETH that the Mortgagors do hereby covenant and agree with and bind and oblige themselves with the Mortgagee that the Mortgagors shall and will well and truly pay or cause to be paid all and every the sums and sum of money which now are or is or which shall or may at any time and from time to time and at all times hereafter be or become due owing and payable to the Mortgagee from and by the Mortgagors under and in terms of these presents together with interest costs and other charges. 
AND as security for such payment as aforesaid to the Mortgagee:
FIRSTLY - the Mortgagors do hereby specially mortgage and hypothecate to and with the said Mortgagee as a primary mortgage free from any and all seizures charges liens and encumbrances or claims whatsoever the entirety of the Building Unit described in the Schedule hereto together with the estate right title interest property claim and demand whatsoever of the Mortgagors in to out of or upon the same (hereinafter referred to as “the said property hereby mortgaged and hypothecated”).
SECONDLY – the Mortgagors do hereby cede assign set over and assure with the said Mortgagee by way of a good and valid primary mortgage and hypothecation free from any and all seizures charges liens encumbrances all sums of money which may be recoverable or receivable under all and every contracts and contract of insurance already effected or which may hereafter from time to time be effected in respect of the said property hereby mortgaged and hypothecated and all the estate right title interest property claim and demand whatsoever of the Mortgagors in to out of or upon the same
(All of which property above referred to and hereby mortgaged hypothecated and assigned are together hereinafter referred to as “the mortgaged property”).
AND the Mortgagors do hereby covenant and agree with the said Mortgagee as follows:
(1) that the Mortgagors have good and legal right and full power and authority to mortgage hypothecate and assign the said mortgaged property in the manner aforesaid and that the same is the absolute property of and is at the full disposal of the Mortgagors and is not subject to any proceedings for acquisition or requisition by the Government or any State sponsored Corporation or other authority and that the said mortgaged property are not subject to any seizure charge lien or encumbrance whatsoever and that the Mortgagors shall and will at the request of the said Mortgagee but at the cost and expense of the Mortgagors do and execute or cause to be done and executed all such further and other acts deeds matters assurances and things for the more perfectly and effectually assuring to the said Mortgagee by way of primary mortgage hypothecation and assignment as aforesaid the mortgaged property or any part or portion thereof as by the said Mortgagee shall or may be reasonably required;
(2) that the Mortgagors shall not pledge mortgage hypothecate assign or create any lien or charge on the mortgaged property or any part or portion thereof and shall not and will not suffer any part or portion thereof to be seized or taken in execution of any judgement/s or under or in respect of any other claim/s or proceedings whatsoever; 
(3) that the Mortgagors shall and will at all times keep and maintain the said mortgaged property and every part or portion thereof in a fit and proper state of management repair and condition and shall and will permit any person/s duly authorized by the said Mortgagee from time to time to visit and inspect the state and order of the said mortgaged property and every part or portion thereof;
4) that the Mortgagors shall and will deliver to the said Mortgagee on the last day of each and every month a duly authenticated statement of the monies spent on the development of the said mortgaged property hereby assigned or expressed or intended so to be and which come under the operation of these Presents; 
(5) that the Mortgagors shall and will at all times regularly and punctually pay the rates taxes assessments charges premia duties levies impositions outgoings and expenses whatsoever in respect of the said mortgaged property and procure official receipts therefore and shall and will whenever required to do so by the said Mortgagee forthwith deliver the said official receipts or any other writings documents relative to or as proof of any such payments aforesaid;
(6) that the Mortgagors shall and will at all times at their own cost and expense insure and keep insured in the joint names of the Mortgagors as the Owner in respect of the said mortgaged property and the said Mortgagee against loss damage or destruction by fire lightning explosion malicious agency riots and strikes and such other causes and contingencies of the said mortgaged property to the full insurable value thereof with any insurer and shall and will regularly and punctually pay all and every the premia and premium or sums or sum of money for the time being necessary for keeping on foot the said policy or policies of insurance and shall and will whenever requested by the said Mortgagee forthwith deliver the receipts for the same and shall and will observe and comply with all and singular the terms and conditions and provisions of such policy or policies of insurance;
(7) that in case the said mortgaged property or any part or portion thereof be burnt down destroyed by fire or lightning or explosion or malicious agency or riots or strikes or any other cause or contingency insured against then and in any such case and as often as the same shall happen all such sums or sum of money as shall be received or be recoverable by virtue of any such insurance or insurances shall be paid by the Mortgagors to the said Mortgagee and shall at the option of the said Mortgagee be applied towards the discharge or part discharge of the moneys then due owing and payable by the Mortgagors to the said Mortgagee under or in respect of these Presents;
(8) That the Mortgagors shall and will during the continuance of the mortgage and hypothecation effected by these Presents duly and faithfully observe perform and comply with the obligations undertakings agreements and covenants on the part of the Mortgagors contained in these Presents; 
(9) Until there is a default and unless otherwise directed by the said Mortgagee the Mortgagors shall be at liberty in the ordinary course of business to sell all or any of the mortgaged property with the written consent of the said Mortgagee within the operation of these Presents. In the event of the mortgage and hypothecation and assignment created by these Presents being enforced and if the Mortgagors are called upon to do so the Mortgagors shall and will upon receipt of the proceeds of every such sale or recovery pay the same forthwith to the said Mortgagee in or towards the discharge or part discharge of the monies then due owing and payable by the Mortgagors to the said Mortgagee in respect of these Presents; 

AND it is hereby expressly agreed and declared as follows:

(a) that if the Mortgagors shall fail or neglect to keep the mortgaged property insured as aforesaid or deliver to the said Mortgagee the policy or policies of insurance or the receipts for the payment of premia as aforesaid or shall fail or neglect to duly pay the rates taxes assessments and other outgoings or to deliver to the said Mortgagee the official receipts thereof as hereinbefore provided or shall fail or neglect to pay any other sums or sum of money payable in fulfillment of the covenants herein contained then and in any such case it shall be lawful for but not obligatory on the said Mortgagee to pay all such premia duties outgoings and expenses and all costs payments and expenses for effecting or keeping on foot such insurances and rents rates taxes assessments and other outgoings and such other sums or sum of money aforesaid and the same together with interest thereon at the rate or rates in these Presents provided/directed shall on demand be payable to the said Mortgagee ( as the case may be) and be recoverable from the Mortgagors and shall be charged specially upon the mortgaged property and shall be covered by the mortgage hypothecation and assignment hereby given and granted or expressed or intended so to be;
(b) that the acceptance by the said Mortgagee of the security created and effected by these Presents shall in no way or manner prejudice or affect any lien to which the said Mortgagee is by law entitled or any other securities which the said Mortgagee already hold or may at any time and from time to time hereafter hold from or on account of the Mortgagors;
(c) that if the Mortgagors shall make default in the payment of any money hereby secured or shall commit a breach of any of the covenants herein contained and on the part of the Mortgagors to be kept done observed and performed or if the Mortgagors shall at any time during the continuance of the mortgage and hypothecation effected by these Presents be wound up be liquidated or enter into a composition with their respective creditors or if proceedings are taken for the winding up of the Mortgagors (whether voluntary or otherwise) or if the mortgaged property or any part or portion thereof is seized or taken in execution or similar proceedings which may be available under the law of Sri Lanka or if the Mortgagors shall cease to carry on business or if any circumstance shall arise which in the reasonable opinion of the said Mortgagee may endanger the security constituted by these Presents then and in any such case it shall be lawful for the said Mortgagee to take all such steps as the said Mortgagee consider necessary to protect his interests and to enter upon and take possession of the said mortgaged property or any part or portion thereof and of the documents of title contracts and other documents whatsoever relating to the said mortgaged property with full power to sell or dispose of the same or any part or portion thereof and all costs and expenses of such sale or disposal or in any way incidental thereto (all of which costs and expenses shall be charged specially upon the mortgaged property and shall be covered by the mortgage hypothecation and assignment hereby given and granted or expressed or intended so to be) have been fully paid and liquidated or to exercise the powers and remedies available to the said Mortgagee under any law or legislation in Sri Lanka or at once to sue for and recover payment from the Mortgagors of all moneys due under or secured by these Presents and to obtain a hypothecary decree in respect of the said mortgaged property hereby mortgaged against the Mortgagors and to take such and all other steps to protect the interests of the said Mortgagee or if the said Mortgagee shall think fit so to do also at any time to adopt the above remedies simultaneously or successively for recovering payment of the said moneys and in the event of the said Mortgagee suing for the recovery of the moneys aforesaid the Mortgagors do hereby expressly agree that the said Mortgagee shall be entitled to have a Receiver appointed in respect of the mortgaged property pending such action who in addition to the powers and authorities conferred by law or by a competent court shall be vested with all the rights powers and authorities hereby given to the said Mortgagee without prejudice however to the right hereby given to the said Mortgagee to enter upon and take possession of the property hereby mortgaged with all the rights powers and authorities hereinbefore given;
(d) this Deed shall be security to the said Mortgagee for the balance due or from time to time due by the Mortgagors to the said Mortgagee in relation to the monies due owing and payable by the Mortgagors in terms of these presents it being intended that the total amount of the monies hereby secured or to be ultimately recoverable by the said Mortgagee in terms of these presents from and out of the security created hereby shall not exceed the sum of Rupees Ten million (Rs. 10,000,000/-) of lawful money of Sri Lanka and interest costs expenses and other charges thereon; 
(e) any statement extract writing or other documents showing the amounts due by the Mortgagors under these presents and made out of the books or documents of the said Mortgagee and signed and certified by the said Mortgagee or by any person/s who may be specially appointed for that purpose by the said Mortgagee shall be presumed to be correct in the absence of manifest error;
(f) that every notice given or demand made under this Deed and every summons or notice of any kind whatsoever in connection with any action suit or proceedings taken under these Presents shall be taken and deemed to have been duly served upon the Mortgagors if the same be sent by post under registered cover addressed to the respective Mortgagors at the respective addresses hereinbefore mentioned or to the registered office for the time being of the Mortgagors; 
(i) That this Deed shall be governed by and construed according to the laws of Sri Lanka.
