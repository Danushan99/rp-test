WHEREAS the VENDOR is seised and possessed of the Condominium Parcel more fully described in the Schedule hereto, being Apartment No. B-12 on the 12th Floor of the building known as "Royal Towers" situated at No. 85, Galle Road, Colombo 04, by virtue of Deed of Transfer No. 884 dated 20.06.2018 attested by P. AND WHEREAS the VENDOR has agreed with the PURCHASER to sell and convey the said Condominium Parcel more fully described in the Schedule hereto unto the Purchaser.
- : W I T N E S S E T H:-
The VENDOR shall sell and the PURCHASER shall purchase the Condominium Parcel described in the Schedule hereto free of encumbrances for the price of RUPEES THIRTY-FIVE MILLION (Rs.35,000,000/-).
The PURCHASER shall pay to the VENDOR a sum of RUPEES THREE MILLION FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND (Rs.3,500,000/-) as an advance at the execution of this Agreement, the receipt whereof the VENDOR doth hereby acknowledge.
On the payment of the balance purchase price of RUPEES THIRTY-ONE MILLION FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND (Rs.31,500,000/-) to the VENDOR by the PURCHASER within three (03) months from the date hereof, the VENDOR shall execute a Deed of Transfer conveying the said premises to the PURCHASER.
On the execution of this Agreement, the VENDOR shall not encumber the said premises by pledging, mortgaging or otherwise.
The VENDOR doth hereby declare that he has good and valid title to convey the said premises, that there are no encumbrances or liens affecting the said premises, and that there are no pending cases or proceedings affecting the same.
The VENDOR shall pay all condominium management fees, rates, taxes, and utility bills up to the date of execution of the final Deed of Transfer.
Should the VENDOR fail to execute the Deed of Transfer upon the PURCHASER tendering the balance sum, the PURCHASER shall be entitled to enforce specific performance of this agreement or recover the advance paid together with interest at the rate of Twenty Percentum (20%) per annum.
Should the PURCHASER fail to complete the purchase within the stipulated period, the VENDOR shall be entitled to forfeit the entirety of the advance payment of RUPEES THREE MILLION FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND (Rs.3,500,000/-) made hereunder.
