Herath, Licensed Surveyor under and by virtue of Deed No. 1550 dated 20.01.2006 attested by J.B. Aluwihare, Notary Public which is morefully described in the schedule hereto.
AND WHEREAS the said VENDOR has agreed with DAVID CHEN (Holder of National Identity Card No. 755432109V) of No. 7, Gregory's Road, Colombo 7 in the said Republic of Sri Lanka (hereinafter sometimes called and referred to as the VENDEE which term shall where the context so requires or admits mean and include the said DAVID CHEN for the absolute sale and transfer unto the said VENDEE and their heirs, executors, administrators and assignees all that divided and defined allotment of land which is morefully described in the schedule hereto for a sum of RUPEES TWENTY FIVE MILLION (Rs. 25,000,000/=) of lawful money of the Republic of Sri Lanka.
NOW KNOW YE AND THESE PRESENTS WITNESS that the said VENDOR in pursuance of the said Agreement and in consideration of the said sum of RUPEES TWENTY FIVE MILLION (Rs. 25,000,000/=) of lawful money of the said Republic of Sri Lanka well and truly paid to the VENDOR by the said Vendee (the receipt whereof the VENDOR do hereby expressly admit and acknowledge) doth hereby sell, grant, convey, assign, transfer, set over and assure unto the said VENDEE the said allotment of land morefully described in the Schedule hereto together with all and singular the rights, ways, privileges, easements advantages, servitudes and appurtenances whatsoever thereunto belonging or appertaining or usually held used occupied enjoyed therewith or reputed or known as part and parcel thereof and all the estate right title interest property benefit claim and demand whatsoever of the said VENDOR into upon or out of the said allotment of land and every part thereof.
TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the said allotment of land hereby sold granted conveyed and assured or expressed or intended so to be together with all the rights and appurtenances thereunto belonging unto the VENDEE absolutely and forever.
AND the said VENDOR doth hereby covenant promise and agree with the said VENDEE, that the said VENDEE shall and may at all times hereafter peaceably and quietly possess and enjoy the said allotments of land hereby sold and conveyed and receive the rents income issues and profits thereof without any interruption or disturbance by the said VENDOR or any other person or persons whomsoever lawfully claiming any right or title thereto and that the said VENDOR has good right and full power and lawful and absolute authority to transfer and convey the said allotment of land morefully described in the Schedule hereto in the manner aforesaid and that the same are free from all encumbrances whatsoever and not subject to any mortgage charge lien seizure sequestration partition or other proceedings or encumbrance whatsoever.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF the said VENDOR doth hereunto and to two others from the same tenor and date as these presents set his hand at Kandy on this Tenth (10th) day of November Two Thousand and Nineteen (2019).
