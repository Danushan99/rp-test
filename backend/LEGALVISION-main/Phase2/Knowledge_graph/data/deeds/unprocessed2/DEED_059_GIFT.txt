WHEREAS the VENDORS are seised and possessed of the land and premises described in the Schedule hereto by virtue of Deed of Gift No. 1500 dated 05.03.1995 attested by S. Perera, Notary Public.
AND WHEREAS the VENDORS have agreed to sell and the PURCHASER has agreed to purchase the said land and premises for the consideration and upon the terms and conditions hereinafter appearing.
- : W I T N E S S E T H:-
The VENDORS shall sell and the PURCHASER shall purchase all that land and premises described in the Schedule hereto free from all encumbrances for the sum of RUPEES FIFTY-TWO MILLION FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND (Rs.52,500,000/-).
The PURCHASER shall pay to the VENDORS a sum of RUPEES FIVE MILLION (Rs.5,000,000/-) as an advance upon the execution of this Agreement.
The PURCHASER shall pay the balance sum of RUPEES FORTY-SEVEN MILLION FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND (Rs.47,500,000/-) within four (04) months from the date hereof, upon which the VENDORS shall execute a valid Deed of Transfer in favour of the PURCHASER or his nominee.
The VENDORS hereby covenant not to sell, lease, mortgage or otherwise encumber the said property during the subsistence of this Agreement.
The VENDORS shall furnish to the PURCHASER, within thirty (30) days from date hereof, all original title deeds, an up-to-date survey plan, and certificates of non-vesting and street line from the Kotte Municipal Council.
Upon receipt of the advance payment, the VENDORS shall grant access to the PURCHASER and his agents for the purpose of inspection and valuation, upon prior notice.
If the VENDORS default in their obligations, the PURCHASER shall be entitled to sue for specific performance or claim a refund of the advance with a penalty of an equivalent sum as liquidated damages.
If the PURCHASER defaults in completing the transaction, the VENDORS shall be entitled to forfeit the advance sum of RUPEES FIVE MILLION (Rs.5,000,000/-) paid hereunder.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF the parties have placed their signatures on the date and at the place first hereinabove mentioned.
